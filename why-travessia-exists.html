Why Travessia Exists

Essential industries move enormous volumes of value every day. These businesses operate on tight margins, predictable settlement cycles, and constant capital turnover.

Despite their importance, they are structurally underserved by traditional credit systems. Banks are slow, collateral heavy, and prone to withdrawing credit precisely when it is needed most.

At the same time, crypto capital is abundant but largely optimized for volatility and market exposure rather than real economic throughput.

Travessia exists to align these two worlds. It uses on-chain infrastructure to route global liquidity into real world essential industries, where capital is immediately productive and continuously recycled.

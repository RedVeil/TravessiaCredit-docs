Overview

Travessia Credit is an on-chain credit protocol that uses programmable infrastructure to finance real world operations in essential industries.

Users deposit stablecoins into smart contract vaults that deploy capital into short duration credit loans.

In return, users earn yield generated directly by real world economic activity from partners. This gives real businesses access to credit and vault users predictable, uncorrelated, and crisis resistant yield.
